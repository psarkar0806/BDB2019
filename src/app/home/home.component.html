After eight successful years of hosting Durga Puja and connecting New England communities, Boston Durga Bari welcomes you to yet another fun-packed five-day long event. As usual, our goal is to foster an open and collaborative platform for patrons to connect at various levels - which encourages us to keep the event non-ticketed, albeit your donations help to keep the wheels spinning. Please review the calendar of events and timing for each day below, but do not hesitate to join in the uber-casual group chats, fondly called adda, while you sip on the refreshments in the pandal on any day/time
