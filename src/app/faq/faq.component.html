Q1: This is a 5-day event. Can I attend on any day??

Please be a part of us on any day of the event.

Q2: Do I have to pay to have food (Bhog)?

NO. We have moved away from being a ticketed event. You may sponsor for a puja day or help us with a donation to cover our costs.

Q3: Are you open all day during the five days? Why is the event running beyond the weekend?

We make a humble attempt to align our dates with the actual dates of Durga Puja in India. Hence our days are not restricted to weekends

Q4: Do I have to be in an invitation guest list in order to attend?

While that is not 'required', but if you are planning to attend, we would love to hear from you, get a chance to know you. Please drop us a note.

Q5: What is the menu for the meals (Bhog)??

Menu is solely decided by our volunteers. We promise a sumptuous hot meal.

Q6: When will bhog be served?

In the afternoon(only on Saptami and Ashtami) around 1 pm and in the evening(all days) around 8pm. We will publish the exact timings on our home page shortly

Q7: How can I participate in the cultural, puja or bhog?

We welcome all volunteers. Please contact any of our committee members. They will be more than happy to accomodate you.

Q8: Do I need to pay?

NO. We have moved away from being a ticketed event. You may sponsor for a puja day or help us with a donation to cover our costs.

Q9: Can I sponsor specific portions or sub-events of the day?

Please refer to our donation page for details.

Q10: Is parking available on site?

Please refer to the parking details on our home page for details.

Q11: Are there dress codes to attend the event?

Well, we will be flauting our Indian attire. Please feel free to dress comfortably.

Yes, we have provisioned for a heated tent and all other equipments that are necessary to maintain a room temperature within the facility.
