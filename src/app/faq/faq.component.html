Q1: What are the attendance guidelines ?

Due to the recent pandemic, we have outlined some guidelines to be followed to ensure safety of attending patrons and volunteers. During the event, we will have professional deep cleaning of the premises including disinfectant foggers, UV treatment on a daily basis. In lieu of this, we have outlined the below guiding principles that patrons and volunteers should adhere to:

Pre-registration is required. Pre-registration form will be available in our website (www.bostondurgabari.com) starting October 1, 2021

We will limit attendance inside the pandal to ~100 people at a time

Everyone needs to wear masks adhering to CDC guidelines, at all times, except eating/drinking/photos.

Everyone above 12 years may need to show proof of vaccination, if required
