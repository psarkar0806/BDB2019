First of all, thanks to all our donors, sponsors and members for continuing to support the event and inherent causes. When we began our humble journey nine years ago, our goal was to find an opportunity to get like-minded professionals of the area together. But as we learned the ropes and found meaning in the celebration of an auspiscious event, we got ambitious. We grew exponentially in terms of membership, expanded to include wider communities and began to imbibe in social causes globally. In search for the perfect re-creation of our home-town puja expereince, we decided to host the event in a tent (Bengali's call it a Pandal), and started cooking the meals (Bhog) in-house ourselves. With this, came the eternal Bengali pass-time called Adda. Two years ago, we took an audacious step - of making the event a Non-Ticketed one, and only depended on optional member donations and kind sponsorships. Last year, we were humbled to have hosted more than 2200 patrons through the event days. Please feel free to check out last few year's festivities In our ninth year, we are taking bolder footsteps to grow in member base and an even more enriched experience for the participants.

Hope to see you with your friends and family.
